Important Announcement: Rain World Discontinuation

A Sad Farewell to Rain World

It is with a heavy heart that we announce the discontinuation of Rain World. After much deliberation, the team at Videocult has made the difficult decision to bring the game's development to a close. As of 03/04/25, Rain World will no longer receive updates, patches, or additional content.

While this marks the end of an era, we are deeply grateful to our loyal community for supporting the game through its highs and lows.

What Does This Mean for You?

  • No More Updates: No new updates or patches will be released, including any potential bug fixes or quality of life improvements. We advise players to enjoy the current state of the game as it stands.
  • Servers Offline: The online multiplayer and server features will be shut down on [insert date]. This includes access to leaderboards, multiplayer matches, and any community-driven online events. Please ensure that you have completed any necessary in-game activities before then.
  • Store Removal: Rain World will be removed from all digital platforms, including Steam, PlayStation Store, and others, on [insert date]. If you already own the game, it will remain in your library, but no new purchases will be possible.

A Heartfelt Thanks from Videocult

We want to take a moment to express our deepest gratitude to all the fans of Rain World. Your passion, creativity, and dedication to the game have made this journey unforgettable. The Rain World community has grown into something truly special, and we thank each and every one of you for your support.

Closing Message

Although Rain World may be coming to an end, the impact it has made on players, developers, and the gaming community will never fade. The world of the slugcat will always remain in our hearts, and we hope that Rain World continues to inspire future games and projects.

Thank you for everything.
